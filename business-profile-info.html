About Us

Ready for some fitness fun on the beach? Kite Beach is the home to one of three Fitness Villages in 2021 and the massive area will be full of workout opportunities for every level. From beachside activities like volleyball and beach football, to HIIT workouts, trampoline workouts and even a ladies-only area, theres loads to do. Bringing the gang along? The Teen Fit area will host a variety of boot camps, dance classes and functional training for 13 to 18 year-olds and the Kids Camp has a Skill & Play area with monkey bars and climbing challenges to keep your little ones active.

Free entry with pre registration preferred and onsite registration available if necessary.
